Про проект:

Is a spin-off trading card game based on the World Wide mythology. The game will focus on collecting cards, building decks with various strategies and dueling other players online. The goal of the game is to evolve the digital TCG experience in never seen before visual presentation on mobiles.
The player is given a card deck with Gods. With each victory against opponents, new cards are found or can be purchased and the deck will grow stronger with new and more powerful cards added to it. The game will also feature online multiplayer against real opponents and social elements for sharing your progress and finding new friends through Facebook.
Along with general trade mechanics would be using cryptocurrency wallet by using which you would have a possibility to trade/buy or sell collectible cards or boosters using cryptocurrency.
